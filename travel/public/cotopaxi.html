Cotopaxi

Tour Description

Cotopaxi is an active volcano located in the Andes Mountains of Ecuador. It is the second-highest volcano in Ecuador, after Chimborazo, and is one of the most active volcanoes in the world. Cotopaxi is a stratovolcano, which means that it is made up of layers of ash, lava, and rock.

  • Pickup from your hotel in Quito in the morning or haciendas close to Cotopaxi National Park.
  • Quito hotel in Ecuador.
  • Drive: Drive to Cotopaxi National Park, which takes about 2 hours.
  • Cotopaxi National Park in Ecuador.
  • Stop 1: Visit the Limpiopungo Lagoon, a beautiful glacial lake located at the foot of the volcano.
  • Stop 2: Hike to the José Rivas Refuge, which is located at an altitude of 4,864 meters (15,927 feet).
  • José Rivas Refuge in Ecuador.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a delicious lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Optional: Go horseback riding or mountain biking in the park.
  • Drive back: Drive back to Quito or hacienda in the afternoon.
